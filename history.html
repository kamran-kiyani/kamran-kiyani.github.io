The Main Character: Tis Mar Khan

Muhammad Khan, a poor village tailor from Saidpur journeys through pre-mutiny India in search of his cousin. His encounters with friends and foes along the way seal his reputation as Tis Mar Khan (The chief who defeats 30 at a time). His adventures become known far and wide before he is thrown into the midst of the Indian mutiny against the British East India Company. His character is put to test before he proves himself worthy of the title.

The region and setting

  • Saidpur, a quiet village by the Margalla hills
  • Along the caravan route from Peshawar to Delhi
  • Located in Potohar, a dusty backwater region north of the Punjab
  • Inhabited by tribes loyal to the Mughal emperor
  • Famous towns along this route: Rawalpindi, Lahore, Amritsar, Delhi.
